# NODE Members Wiki, Full Content Draft

*Draft for review. Nothing here is published. This is the complete member-facing content for a members-only wiki, curated and rewritten from the Node Notion. Once you've marked it up, the next step is building it into the site (`/dashboard/wiki`).*

This draft covers **24 pages across 7 sections**. It pulls in every evergreen/reusable page identified in the audit (`WIKI_CONTENT_PLAN.md`) and deliberately leaves out year-specific logistics and anything containing personal data (rosters, addresses, phone numbers, door codes, etc.), those stay in Notion.

### How to review

Read top to bottom, or jump to a section. Everything is written for a logged-in NODE member ("you"). Watch for three kinds of markers:

- **[NEEDS UPDATE: …]**, a specific value or link the camp must fill in or refresh (usually a year-specific number or a current link). All of these are collected into a punch list in the closing appendix.
- **[TODO: re-host …]**, an image or asset that lived in Notion and must be re-hosted in the app, because Notion's image links expire. No Notion images are linked directly.
- `<!-- editor: … -->` and `<!-- SOURCE: … -->`, my notes and the source Notion page ID. These are HTML comments; they won't render to members.
