## Comms Archive (recommendation, not imported)

NODE's Notion holds two running communication logs. Neither is drafted into the wiki, on purpose:

- **Camp Email Archive**, ~40 dated camp-wide blast emails (2022-2026). Useful as a "what we communicate, and when" reference, but bulky and year-bound. **Recommendation:** if you want it accessible, add a single wiki page that links out to the Notion archive rather than copying 40 emails into the site.
- **Camp Call Archive**, meeting notes from monthly calls. **Not imported and not recommended for import:** it contains a home address, Zoom recording passwords, and ad-hoc planning chatter. Leave it in Notion behind its existing access controls.

Everything sensitive from the archives (the ~50-person camper registry, placement applications, lodging addresses, itineraries) was intentionally excluded from this draft. See `WIKI_CONTENT_PLAN.md` §5 for the full do-not-publish list.
